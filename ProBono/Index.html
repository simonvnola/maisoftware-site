A different kind of giving app

Most volunteering asks you to do something outside your core skill set. ProBono flips that. It connects your day job to your generosity, so your giving feels practical, repeatable, and meaningful.

Your work becomes the trigger

Choose a recurring hour in your week and use it as the anchor for your giving habit.

Notifications that matter

Get reminders before your hour begins, at the start, and when it ends so the act of giving stays connected to the work itself.

Track effort and impact

What makes ProBono useful

ProBono is designed to make generosity easier to sustain over time, especially for people whose work is already demanding.

  • Simple weekly pledge setup
  • Recurring reminders tied to real calendar moments
  • Donation and hour tracking in one app
  • Clear summaries for personal reflection and tax-time record keeping
ProBono does not process donations itself. Donations are made directly through third-party charity pages or providers selected by the user.
