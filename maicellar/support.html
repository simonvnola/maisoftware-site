Tips & tricks

Get better results

Good lighting

Scan labels in natural light or under a bright lamp. Avoid flash directly on the label as it creates glare.

Fill the frame

Get close enough that the label fills most of the camera frame. The AI reads better with larger text.

Voice pairing

Be specific — “pan-seared salmon with lemon butter sauce” works better than just “fish”.

Invoice scanning

Photograph the full invoice flat on a table. Multi-page invoices can be scanned page by page.
