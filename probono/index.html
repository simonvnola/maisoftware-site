Why it works

Your professional hour is worth more than you think

Volunteering your time directly — at a soup kitchen, or on a build project overseas — is a generous act. But for most professionals, donating an hour's earnings to an effective charity creates far more impact than spending that same hour volunteering in person.

A surgeon who donates an hour's pay can fund dozens of surgeries performed by local doctors at a fraction of the cost. The most powerful thing most of us can do isn't to show up with a paintbrush — it's to show up with a paycheck.

In practice

Jenny starts her week by dedicating her first hour on a Monday morning to the Fred Hollows Foundation. By the end of the morning meeting, her single hour's salary has restored sight to three people through cataract surgery — without taking a single day off work.
