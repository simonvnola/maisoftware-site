How the connection works
What is MCP?

MCP (Model Context Protocol) is an open standard developed by Anthropic that lets AI assistants securely connect to apps and services.

When you connect your AI assistant to Maicasa via MCP, your assistant can read your property data, tasks, and budget — and write back to Maicasa when you ask it to save something.

Think of it like giving your AI assistant a secure key to your Maicasa account — one that you can revoke at any time, and that only works when you start a conversation.

Your data is never shared without your permission
Revoke access at any time from Maicasa settings
How it works in practice

You open Claude.ai or ChatGPT and start a conversation. Your AI assistant connects to Maicasa in the background and retrieves the data relevant to your question — your properties, tasks, quotes, or budget.

When you ask it to save something — a new task, a quote, a note — it writes that back to Maicasa directly. You see the change in the app immediately.

The connection is only active during your conversation. When you close the chat, the connection closes too.
