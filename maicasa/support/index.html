Common fixes

App crashes on launch
Force-close and reopen. If it persists, delete and reinstall — your tasks sync from the cloud once you sign in.
Tasks not saving
Check your internet connection. Maicasa syncs to a cloud database and a connection is needed for saves to persist.
Property scan stalled
Each step saves independently — pull to refresh on the property screen, then tap "resume scan" to retry just the failed steps.
Photo not recognised
Make sure the photo is clear and well-lit. HEIC photos from older iPhones can be slow — the app converts them automatically.
