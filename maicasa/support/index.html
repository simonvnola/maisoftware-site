Common fixes

App crashes on launch
Force-close and reopen. If it persists, delete and reinstall — your tasks sync from the cloud.
Tasks not saving
Check your internet connection. Maicasa syncs to a cloud database and requires a connection to save.
Voice input not working
Go to Settings → Maicasa → Microphone and ensure access is allowed. Works best in quiet environments.
Photo not recognised
Ensure the photo is clear and well-lit. Very large files may time out — try a lower-resolution version.
