What Maicasa does
Everything your home needs.
Nothing it doesn't.
Prioritised task list
AI organises your maintenance jobs by urgency, cost, and timing. Know exactly what needs doing now versus what can wait.
Step-by-step AI guides
Tap any task to get a full guide — first steps, safety watch-outs, what trade you need, and what to ask for when getting quotes.
Inspection report import
Upload a building inspection PDF and Maicasa converts every finding into a tracked, prioritised task — instantly.
Budget planner
See your near-term maintenance spend at a glance. AI estimates costs for upcoming jobs so you can plan without surprises.
Photo recognition
Snap a problem area and let AI identify what's wrong, suggest the right trade, and create a task from what it sees.
AI chat assistant
A built-in AI assistant is ready to help the moment you open the app. Ask anything about your home, paste a maintenance list to import it, or connect Maicasa to Claude or ChatGPT for a deeper experience. Learn more →
