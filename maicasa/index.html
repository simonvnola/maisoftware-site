What Maicasa does
Everything your home needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Six tabs. One coherent picture of your property. Each one earns its place — and works the moment you open the app.

Prioritised task list
Every job ranked by safety, urgency, season and cost. Open the app, glance at Now, and know exactly what to act on first.
Property scan
Address in, tasks out. Maicasa researches your property — facts, council, climate risks, ongoing costs — then generates a tailored maintenance plan.
Inspection report import
Drop in a building inspection PDF and every finding becomes a tracked, prioritised task — instantly. No more re-typing 60-page reports.
Budget planner
FY-aware recurring + contingency budget. AI estimates costs for upcoming jobs and warns you, gently, if you're heading over.
Photo recognition
Snap a damp patch, a cracked tile, a leaky tap. AI identifies the issue, suggests the right trade, and creates a task with cost guidance.
Contents inventory
Every appliance, fixture and fitting, photographed and tagged. Track current value, warranty, and whether it's depreciable for tax.
Trade guides & quote tools
Tap any task: get a step-by-step DIY guide, or the exact brief to hand a tradesperson. Compare quotes side-by-side and flag what's out of range.
AI chat assistant
Ask anything about your home — paste a maintenance list to import it, describe a problem, or get advice at 10pm when something looks wrong.
